    Dettaglio art

    Dettaglissima is a place dedicated to self-expression, where everything is acceptable and there is no ego or attitude. Every client is treated as an equal, and given our full respect and attention.


    We combine old-school gift ideals with modern equipment and the highest safety standards. Our artists are fully expert, highly experienced and dedicated. We're here to answer any question you have, big or small. 

    MORE ABOUT US

    Custom dettagli

    Every dettagli that we create is unique and one-of-a-kind. WE'll start with a design or idea, and create a dettaglio of what you want. When you're happy with it, we're happy with it. There are no shortcuts in our creation process and we pay complete attention to every stage and element. Dettagli are our passion, and our profession.
